Welcome

Welcome to Inshar Ul Haq & Company.

We are a family-run residential construction business, built on more than 40 years of hands-on experience designing and building homes.

Led by Mian Inshar Ul Haq, our team specialises in designing and building premium residential homes from the ground up — structure, interior, and exterior — delivered fully furnished and ready to move into. Every project is overseen personally by the family, not handed off to subcontractors, so the same care that goes into the foundation goes into the final coat of paint.

Structural design is led by Mian Inshar Ul Haq, with interiors and exteriors shaped by Irum Inshar — meaning every home carries one consistent vision from the ground up to the last light fitting.

An established name in Bahria Enclave, Islamabad, we've built a reputation for A+ quality construction, premium finishes, and homes handed over complete — with no need for a single follow-up job.
